Description


  • Are you craving an escape from corporate routine, but are bound by its essential income?
  • Do you delight in breaking things, but the cost of replacing them is becoming a concern?
  • Do you despise your boss, but the constraints of law restrain your violent daydreams?

Unleash your pent-up rage in this chaotic sandbox of destruction; optimized for relaxation.


Sue your way to prosperity! Break safety regulations and file lawsuits to earn Star Bucks!


Spend your Star Bucks on destructive / delicious items to become the ultimate consumer!


Turn trash to treasure! Recycling is effortless when every item is crafted from microplastic!


Make memorable memos: send profanity-laden resignation letters to despised supervisors.


Revitalize your surroundings – if it's not securely anchored, consider it open to relocation.


Replay all day. Explore randomized environments with a lineup of whimsical characters.


Make a News Debut! Our corporate journalist delves into the aftermath of your decisions.
