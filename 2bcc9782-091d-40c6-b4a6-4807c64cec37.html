Description

Gobble, gobble, gulp! Who's munching Power Pellets? Who's zipping around the maze at top speed, chomping up ghosts and scoring big points? It's Pac-Man, the world's hungriest character! And he's got the munchies! Control the iconic Pac-Man as you navigate through mazes, eating Power Dots while simultaneously avoiding the Ghosts. Devour a fruit treat and get a big bonus score. But if you're caught by the spooky hunters roaming the maze, you're ghostflakes! Use the warp tunnel for a quick escape, and swallow up a Power Pellet to temporarily make Pac-Man an unstoppable eater!
